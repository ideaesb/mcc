NEW CARPET INSTALLED

On Saturday, August 07, 2010, the Maui Cricket Club refreshed our cricket pitch with a new carpet. Much Mahalo to County of Maui, Department of Parks and Recreation, for facilitating materials. Kudos to club members who were able to keep up the promise of on-site installation at Baldwin, in spite of everyone's busy schedules. Participating today were Wakhimba Mills, Daniel Lovell, Baz Cumberhatch, Jai Richmond, Chris Sutherland, Gary Richmond, John Harrison, Luke Patel, Uday Kari and, special welcome to Shiheed Sutton.
